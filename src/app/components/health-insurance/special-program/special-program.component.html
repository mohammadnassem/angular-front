Special Programs

The Dubai Health Authority provides specialized programs aimed at providing patients with more intensive care, such as the “Ejada” program, and specialized programs to support patients in accordance with the controls and standards approved by it in this regard, such as the “Basma” program.

EJADA Program

It is a mechanism of modern techniques and tools to measure health quality indicators in the Emirate of Dubai for the purpose of providing high-quality services in line with global indicators while reducing expenses and measuring the extent of patient benefit. Recently, work has been done to employ artificial intelligence technology to identify members of society most at risk of contracting diseases before they occur and reduce the complications of the disease in the event of infection.

BASMA Program

The “Basma” program was launched in January 2018 to support cancer and hepatitis C patients, with the aim of reducing cancer deaths and ensuring complete recovery from hepatitis C cases in the Emirate of Dubai. In the first phase, the program includes breast cancer, colon cancer, and uterine cancer, which are the most common in the country and are among the types that witness high rates of cure in the case of early detection. And hepatitis C patients (hepatitis B and hepatitis C).

Treatment is provided at specialized medical service provider centers accredited by the Dubai Health Authority.
