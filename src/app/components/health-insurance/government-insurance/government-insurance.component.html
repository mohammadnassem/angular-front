Government Insurance Services

The Emirate of Dubai has unified all government health insurance systems under one umbrella, which is the unified health insurance system “Enaya”, which is for citizens of the Emirate of Dubai, Dubai government employees and their families. This transformation aims to surround insurance beneficiaries with one integrated, more advanced system and enable them to obtain high-quality medical services and high-level health care. The unified government health insurance system provides the required medical services on a large scale, and beneficiaries of the unified system can benefit from those services provided.

Unifying government insurance program “Enaya”

The unified health insurance system “Enaya”, which included “Saada” insurance, and other government insurances. This transformation aims to surround insurance beneficiaries with one integrated, more advanced system, and enable them to obtain high-quality medical services and high-level health care. The unified government health insurance system provides the required medical services on a large scale, through the “Neuron” and “Umbrella” companies.

Beneficiaries of the unified system can visit the Human Resources Department at their employer, or directly contact the Dubai Health Authority to obtain more information.
