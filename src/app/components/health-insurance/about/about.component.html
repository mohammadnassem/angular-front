About Dubai Health Insurance

Overview

Dubai Health Insurance Corporation is an institution affiliated with the Dubai Health Authority, in accordance with Resolution No. (18) of 2018, issued by His Highness Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Crown Prince of Dubai, Chairman of the Executive Council, regarding the approval of the authority’s organizational structure. The Corporation is responsible for managing and supervising health insurance in the Emirate of Dubai, in accordance with applicable legislation, and ensuring its provision for citizens, residents and visitors.
